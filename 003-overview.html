Vincent Van Gogh: A Tribute Website

Project Overview

Vincent van Gogh was a post-impressionist artist known for his vivid and emotive style, as well as his troubled personal life. This tribute website celebrates his art and legacy, showcasing his most iconic paintings and delving into the story behind each one. Through interactive features and multimedia elements, visitors can experience the landscapes, people, and emotions that inspired his art.

This website was created as part of the freeCodeCamp curriculum for the Responsive Web Design Certification. This certification teaches students the fundamentals of web development, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and how to create responsive, user-friendly websites.
